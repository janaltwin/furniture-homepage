Welcome to Kelly Wood Builder

Kelly Wood Builder is a General Contructor founded in Amarillo, Texas and now with offices in McKinney, Texas. We operate throughout the Mildwest region. Started in 1977, we have to specialize in healthcare and retail construction, but remain very active in all types of projects. Our alliances with multiple architectural firms have paved the way to success in Design/Build, Construction Management, and Fast Track construction.
